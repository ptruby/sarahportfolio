Mars Habitat

Thesis Design

The research station is a live/work environment, in order for the astronauts to stay psychologically healthy and work at maximum efficiency without burning out there needs to be a mental break between the workspace and the non-work space. Though due to the weight limitations of many of the rockets, all of the available built space must be efficiently and economically utilized, each crewmember is allotted roughly
4 m3 for relaxing and working space. Designed correctly, this habitat could continue expanding as people traveling to Mars becomes more commonplace. And as Neil Leach says “No overview of developments in any field is complete without a degree
of fantasy. Just as science fiction often informs developments in science itself, so the realm of ‘design fictions’ can also inform design.
