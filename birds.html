Winging It: An Experimental Gallery About Birds

A small group of five designers researched, designed, budgeted, fabricated, and collaborated to develop a cohesive, engaging and educational experience about ornithology, bird research, for the Carnegie Museum of Natural History. The exhibit was designed and fabricated in four months for less than $15,000, and opened September 23, 2010 to positive reviews.
