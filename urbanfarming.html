Urban Farming + Smart Eating

One of the major issues in Cincinnati is the growing food desert, an area with little or no access to fresh and affordable foods needed to maintain a healthy diet. This access can be interpreted in several ways: physical access to shops, financial access, and knowledge of preparation.

This Environmental Learning Center focuses on the healthy food production — growing, cooking, and eating. One can view all parts of the program from the primary promenade. Participants can also use the secondary promenade to get through the space designated by the greenhouse walls.
