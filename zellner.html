The Object City

Peter Zellner Workshop

This workshop set out five conditions for a differential urbanism: an up-to-date approach to city design that produces a traversal relationality between urban figure, field, ground and network. The workshop scrutinized several new and topical urban design approaches that blur figure-field relations via object oriented manipulations, kit-bashing, collage-ing, folding, topological stranding and landform based approaches to urbanism repurposing 93 everyday objects categorized in three sets: domestic objects, urban objects, and functional objects. This was in collaboration with Michael Rogovin and Udo Greinacher.
