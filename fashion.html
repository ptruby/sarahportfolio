Fashion + Architecture

An interdisciplinary collaborative studio between fashion and architecture, this began as a way to discover the design processes that lay between architecture and design. This collaborative studio blended the inspirations (texture, graphics, draping, detail, and structure) of the Master of Architecture and Master of Design students to create a student-designed collection built during the necessary iterative process. My garment within the collection was a draped dress.
