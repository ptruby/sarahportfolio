KPF

I mainly contributed to the design of Office Headquarters located in London, England while working for Kohn Pedersen Fox Associates. With Ko Makabe’s team, I was responsible for creating images of spaces and details. My largest responsibility was the design of an entrance canopy; I created drawings and renderings for presentations to the client. While at KPF, I also provided detail work on a hotel and residence in Worli, Mumbai, India, and I designed reception and concierge desks for a mixed-use redesign for Plaza 66 in Shanghai, China.

Programs used: AutoCAD, Rhino, Grasshopper, 3D Studio Max, V-Ray, Photoshop
