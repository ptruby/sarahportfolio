LIVE/MAKE Industrial Arts Center

The Cincinnati Chapter of the American Institute of Architects in partnership with the Over-the-Rhine Brewery District Community Urban Redevelopment Corporation presented this architectural design competition, which called for a membership based facility that will feature private residences, and light industrial studios and an open workshop. This center will be a place for members to focus on urban living and the customization from evolving tools and modes of production from the past and present.

Inspired to showcase the industrial and organic nature of the two halves of the center, the building is made up of a very rectilinear cold industrial workspace interrupted by a large organic waffle structure.
