Alien Invasion

This is a concept for an exhibit about extraterrestrial life. Are aliens out there? The exhibit explores the subject from four different points of view: a skeptical astronomer and astrochemist, a recent college graduate, an alien abduction researcher, and a young child. The four characters discuss their thoughts on different objects that have been tied to the extraterrestrial life theory. The written content and replicated artifacts are driven by research into common theories from both skeptical and believer viewpoints.
