ZGF Architects

Part of a team tasked to design the Oregon Health & Science University Center for Health and Healing, a teaching hospital, in Portland, Oregon while working for Zimmer Gunsul Frasca Architects, LLP. I worked on Block 28: the conference center, the above-ground parking structure, and the patient housing/Ronald McDonald House. For this project I designed and modeled skin facades iterations, maximized the parking lot layout, set up packages for the skybridge and tunnel encroachment applications. In the day to day I helped update and maintain the revit model. I also aided in programming meetings for the entrance areas and providing material and diagrams for the Portland Design Advice Review.
