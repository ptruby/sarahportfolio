Soundscape

Suspended Acoustic Ceiling Panels

The Niehoff Studio presented a competition that called for entries that solved
the reverberating acoustic problems of the space. As a small group, we proposed
a paneled cloud system suspended between fluorescent light fixtures that would break the space above into distinct acoustic zones.

Inspired by origami, the folding gives the cardboard rigidness and the installation
a minimalistic aesthetic; it is a functional site specific sculptural installation.
