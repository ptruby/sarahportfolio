Good News Bad News

Compares and contrasts two unrelated texts Hipster: Dead End of Western Civilization by Douglas Haddow, originally published in Adbusters Magazine, and Our Only Planet by Barry Katz, published in Dwell Magazine to convey a message about our impact on the world. Inspired by Tibor Kalman the images and the text are juxtaposed to convey the need to care about the world. This perfect-bound book is 94 pages.
