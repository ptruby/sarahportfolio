Shifted Space

Architectural Center

In the center of the architectural mecca of Columbus, Indiana this architectural center allows visitors and scholars alike to learn and research. This center was inspired by the site forces that affect the Columbus downtown, specifically the seasonal flooding and the effect on the building mass and form. The three main programmatic parts: the main gallery space, the visitor’s center and the library
are the safe havens of the building shown in the solid massings. As in flooding
the building elements have moved and shifted from side to side.
