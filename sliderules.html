Slide Rules and the Making of the Modern World

While working for Proun: Exhibit Design Consulting, I worked with Chris Danemayer and the MIT Museum staff to design an exhibit to showcase their vast collection of slide rules.

I worked on the graphic production of the exhibit. The project was budgeted
at $7,500 and Slide Rules and the Making of the Modern World opened
September 23, 2011.
