Gone Voting

This campaign was seen as a way to motivate people to go out and help change the world. With small group of students, at the last national election we got the vote moving in a way in which people could relate. As the group leader I was responsible for directing my idea through to completion. The campaign included door hangers, stickers, tickets, and a website we created at gonevoting.com. This project was a collaboration with Amanda Henderson, Jeremy Wolf, and Jackie Weber.
