Simul-Aging

Legacy Film Festival

The Legacy Film Festival on Aging showcases films from around the world that celebrate older adulthood and deal with the challenges and triumphs of ageing. The Festival had a call for film entries that dispelled stereotypes about aging and older adults, and educate viewers about aging in an entertaining and compelling manner.

Simul-Aging takes five dreams from older adults and contrasts them with existing conditions to make people think about what architecture can do to aid in the inevitable aging of people. Each view reflected one of the video collaborator’s thoughts on aging (in order: Sarah Kusuma, Anjali Patel, Melissa Long, Ellen Crawford, and Chloe Bennett).
