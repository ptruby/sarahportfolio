Boutique Hostel

A boutique hostel inspired by Clyfford Still’s paintings, the rugged mountain of his inspiration has been placed neatly inside a box (building) overlooking Findlay Market in Over the Rhine, downtown Cincinnati. Working with John Edwards, under the guidance of the FRCH Design Worldwide hospitality design group, we researched and designed for Findlay Market.

Programs used: AutoCAD, Rhino, 3D Studio Max, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
