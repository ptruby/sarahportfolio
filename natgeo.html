National Geographic Information Visualization

This is a motion information visualization describing National Geographic magazine and how
it works within a minute timeframe. The pictures, a focus of the magazine, became very important in how I described the function of the magazine.

This video uses images from the January 2008 issue of National Geographic and music from Cirque du Soleil’s Varekai.
